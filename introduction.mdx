## Features

Checkout aggregates different payment methods for your users to complete their on-chain action.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Any payment method" icon="square-1">
    Complete your Checkout with any token on any chain, a card, or a centralized exchange.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Any action" icon="square-2">
    Execute any on-chain action. Common actions include swapping, staking, and depositing into perp exchanges.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Any user" icon="square-3">
    Support web3 native and web2 users with social sign on and gas abstraction.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Easy installation" icon="square-4">
    Go live in under 24h with less than 10 minutes of engineering work.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
